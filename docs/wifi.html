WiFi Setup

Update the WiFi credentials on an already-flashed device. Connect via USB-C, enter your 2.4 GHz network details, and click Connect.

Browser not supported. Web Serial requires Chrome or Edge on desktop. Safari and Firefox are not supported.
⚠️ ESP32-S3 is 2.4 GHz only. 5 GHz networks will fail to connect.
💡 You can also clear WiFi credentials directly on the device by holding Button B until it restarts into provisioning mode.
